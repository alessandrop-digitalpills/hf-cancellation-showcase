Baseline 13.7% vs observed 6.7% (249% of CR). To reconcile, recipe_score is pushed to a −175.8% contribution — larger than the cancel rate itself. R² 0.22 masks an unstable structure. Verdict: park it until Sweden accumulates enough Long-Term history.
